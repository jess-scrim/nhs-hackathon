# 2. Background and Purpose

## Background

Healthcare demand and capacity planning requires accurate forecasting methodologies to support operational delivery, workforce planning, and service sustainability. Paediatric services often present additional forecasting challenges due to variability in attendance patterns, seasonal pressures, demographic changes, and changing clinical pathways.

Time series forecasting methodologies are commonly applied to healthcare datasets to estimate future levels of activity based on historical observations. These methodologies can support planning decisions relating to:
- Service demand.
- Bed occupancy.
- Staffing requirements.
- Appointment capacity.
- Escalation planning.

## Purpose

The purpose of this analysis is to assess which time series forecasting methodology is most suitable for use with paediatric demand and capacity data.

The analysis is intended for both technical and executive audiences and aims to:
- Summarise relevant forecasting methodologies.
- Identify considerations relevant to paediatric datasets.
- Highlight evidence required for methodology selection.
- Support future modelling and implementation decisions.
